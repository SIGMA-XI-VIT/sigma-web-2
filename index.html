The Research Society
@sigmaxi.vit

Sigma Xi - The Scientific Research Honor Society is a non-profit honor society for scientists and engineers founded in 1886 at Cornell University. We at Sigma Xi VIT are proud to be the only torchbearers of this legacy in Asia.

As an exclusive research society in VIT Vellore, we are known to conduct momentous conferences, seminars, webinars, and talk shows. Sigma Xi constantly aims to promote and foster an enhanced research atmosphere among young enthusiasts. With a heritage of more than five years, Sigma Xi VIT is proud of its official running Medium Publication and its members who have multiple research papers, reviews, patents, and projects under their wing. Our goal is to grow further with our community to reach the utopian vision of "Society Towards Research".
